Embodied Curiosity

Note: This topic was mentioned during the call but not discussed in depth.

Curiosity that arises from and is expressed through the body, not just the mind.

From Eve Blossom

Eve Blossom's Somatic Experiencing approach emphasizes:

"When we tune into the subtle signals of the body, curiosity becomes less an effort of the mind and more a natural opening to what is present, possible, and previously unseen."

Bodily curiosity:

  • Feels rather than thinks
  • Senses rather than analyzes
  • Opens rather than forces
  • Emerges from spaciousness

Connection to Place

Sense of Place is inherently embodied:

  • Your body is always somewhere
  • Physical relationship to environment
  • Grounded inquiry
