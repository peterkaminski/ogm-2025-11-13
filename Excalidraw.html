A collaborative online whiteboard tool for visual thinking and real-time ideation.

Victoria's Visual Thinking Emphasis

"Let's use for exploring rather than answering. Let's open as many questions as possible. So if instead of saying, 'education closes curiosity,' we state it as a question: 'Does education close?' Then we will have more things to work with."

This embodied the principle of keeping inquiry open.

Visual Thinking Connection

Gabriele G met Victoria through a visual thinking workshop, suggesting the broader community around these practices.

Benefits for Curiosity

The collaborative whiteboard:

  • Enables non-linear thinking
  • Shows connections visually
  • Allows simultaneous contribution
  • Creates shared artifact of collective thinking
  • Supports multiple perspectives literally on same canvas
