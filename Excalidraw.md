## Benefits for Curiosity

The collaborative whiteboard:
- Enables **non-linear thinking**
- Shows **connections** visually
- Allows **simultaneous contribution**
- Creates **shared artifact** of collective thinking
- Supports **multiple perspectives** literally on same canvas
