## Mission

The Right Question Institute focuses on teaching people - especially those in underserved communities - how to ask questions effectively. This empowers them to:
- Participate more fully in democracy
- Advocate for themselves
- Engage with education
- Navigate systems

## Relevance to Curiosity Discussion

The organization addresses the fundamental skill of **question formation** - something rarely taught in formal education despite being essential to curiosity.
