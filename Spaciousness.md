# Spaciousness

**Note:** This topic was mentioned during the call but not discussed in depth.

From [[Eve Blossom]]'s [[Somatic Experiencing]]: the bodily state that allows curiosity to emerge.

## Eve's Framework

> "Turning tension and overwhelm into spaciousness for new perceptions."

**From:**
- Tension
- Overwhelm  
- Constriction
- Reactivity

**To:**
- Spaciousness
- Openness
- Room for perception
- Capacity for curiosity

Curiosity requires **space** - both mental and physical.
